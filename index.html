Beginner lesson path

A simple 4-week dance routine

This starter path works for most styles and helps learners build rhythm, posture, memory and stage confidence.

Week 1Warm-up, posture, counting beats, basic footwork.
Week 2Add hand movement, expressions, turns and short combinations.
Week 3Practice musicality, transitions, clean endings and confidence.
Week 4Create a 45–60 second routine and perform with energy.
