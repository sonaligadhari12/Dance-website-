Beginner lesson path

Start with a simple 4-week routine

This routine works for most beginner dance styles and helps build rhythm, control and confidence.

Week 1Warm-up, posture, counting beats, basic footwork.
Week 2Add hand movement, turns, expressions and short combinations.
Week 3Practice musicality, transitions, confidence and clean endings.
Week 4Create a 45–60 second routine and perform it with energy.

Culture and meaning

Dance is more than steps.

Every style carries a story: celebration, discipline, identity, rhythm, theatre, fitness or community. This website teaches technique together with meaning, so learners understand both movement and emotion.

Begin with one rhythm, one step, one moment of confidence.
